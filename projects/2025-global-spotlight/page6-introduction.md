# Introduction

# 引言

As part of our ongoing World of Open Source research series, the 2025 edition investigates how open source is adopted across core technology stacks and how organizations employ security evaluation practices, formal governance structures, and support for open source in production environments. The study examines regional variations in open source engagement patterns, the emergence of enterprise-level support expectations that challenge traditional community-driven models, and the competitive advantages gained by organizations that successfully transition from passive consumption to active participation in open source projects.

2025 版的研究作为正在进行的 “开源世界”系列研究的一部分，主要调查了如何在核心技术栈采用开源，以及企业如何在生产环境中采用安全评估实践、正式治理结构和开源支持。该研究探讨了开源参与模式的地区差异、挑战传统社区驱动模式的企业级支持期望的出现，以及成功从被动消费过渡到积极参与开源项目的组织所获得的竞争优势。

This research, based on 851 global survey responses (see Methodology for demographics), reveals a misalignment between adoption and governance, providing insights for technology leaders, executives, and policymakers in an era where open source has become critical to business operations and competitive positioning.

这项研究基于 851 份全球调查反馈（有关人口学统计数据，请参阅“方法”章节），揭示了采用与治理之间的错位，在开源已成为企业运营和竞争定位关键的时代，为技术领导者、高管和政策制定者提供了洞察。
