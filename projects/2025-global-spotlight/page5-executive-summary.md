# Executive summary

# 内容摘要

As we have done for the last three years, the Linux Foundation engaged the open source community in its World of Open Source Survey. In this 2025 edition,we confirm that organizations depend on open source software as the backbone of their critical systems. However, most lack the governance and security frameworks to manage this dependency safely. While expecting enterprise-level reliability and support, organizations systematically underinvest in the security practices, formal governance structures, community engagement, and comprehensive strategies that production environments demand. This misalignment creates business risks and limits competitive advantages.

正如我们在过去三年中所做的那样，Linux 基金会在“全球开源调查”中与开源社区进行了互动。在 2025 年度的调查中，我们确认企业依赖开源软件作为其关键系统的支柱。然而，大多数企业缺乏安全管理这种依赖性的治理和安全框架。在期望获得企业级可靠性和支持的同时，企业对生产环境所需的安全实践、正式治理结构、社区参与和综合战略的投资却系统性地不足。这种错位造成了业务风险，限制了竞争优势。

Despite open source’s widespread adoption in mission-critical infrastructure (with 40-55% penetration across operating systems, cloud platforms, databases, DevOps, and AI) organizational maturity lags behind usage. Only 26% of organizations have implemented Open Source Program Offices (OSPOs) and just 34% have defined clear open source strategies, even though 83% believe open source is valuable to their future and 46% report increased business value from open source over the past year. This governance gap creates substantial risk exposure given the mission-critical nature of these deployments.

尽管开源在关键任务基础设施中得到了广泛采用（在操作系统、云平台、数据库、DevOps 和人工智能中的渗透率为 40-55%），但组织成熟度却落后于使用情况。只有 26% 的组织实施了开源项目办公室 (OSPO)，只有 34% 的组织制定了明确的开源战略，尽管 83% 的组织认为开源对他们的未来很有价值，46% 的组织报告说他们的开源项目增加了。

The security posture is equally concerning. Most organizations fail to implement basic security evaluation practices, with only 31% using automated security testing tools. No single security assurance framework has achieved broad adoption, creating fragmentation that undermines security across the ecosystem. Organizations demonstrate inconsistent evaluation practices, with community health checks (44%) being more common than security assessments of the technology.

安全态势同样令人担忧。大多数组织未能实施基本的安全评估实践，只有 31% 的组织使用自动安全测试工具。没有一个单一的安全保证框架得到广泛采用，这就造成了碎片化，破坏了整个生态系统的安全性。各组织的评估实践不一致，社区健康检查（44%）比技术安全评估更常见。

These deficiencies drive organizations toward commercial support solutions that complement traditional open source dynamics. A majority of surveyed organizations demand enterprise-grade support, with 71% expecting sub-12-hour response times, and consider paid support essential for mission-critical workloads (54%), systems handling sensitive data (43%), and regulated environments (38%). This shift reflects open source’s evolution from a cost-saving alternative to foundational business infrastructure requiring formal service level agreements and dedicated support personnel.

这些缺陷促使企业转向商业支持解决方案，以补充传统的开源动态。大多数受访企业都要求获得企业级支持，71% 的企业希望响应时间低于 12 小时，并认为付费支持对于关键任务工作负载（54%）、处理敏感数据的系统（43%）和受监管环境（38%）至关重要。这一转变反映了开源正从节省成本的替代品演变为需要正式服务水平协议和专职支持人员的基础业务基础设施。

Organizations seeking to capture full value from open source should consider establishing formal governance structures, implementing comprehensive security evaluation frameworks, and transitioning from passive consumption to active participation. Those with active open source participation are 20% more likely to perceive competitive advantages than passive users, while also gaining benefits in workplace satisfaction (78%) and talent attraction (74%).

寻求从开源中获取全部价值的组织应考虑建立正式的管理结构，实施全面的安全评估框架，并从被动消费过渡到主动参与。与被动用户相比，那些积极参与开源的组织更有可能获得 20% 的竞争优势，同时还能在工作场所满意度（78%）和人才吸引力（74%）方面获益。

Systematic investment can help bridge the maturity gap. Priorities focus on sponsoring critical dependencies (44%), training developers (41%), and increasing upstream contributions (39%). Intellectual property concerns (33%) and licensing complexities (37%) remain barriers requiring dedicated expertise and formal governance structures. AI emerges as the technology domain that benefits most from being open source (38% of respondents), with adoption showing a statistically significant 5% increase from 2024.

系统性投资有助于缩小成熟度差距。优先考虑的重点是赞助关键依赖（44%）、培训开发人员（41%）和增加上游贡献（39%）。知识产权问题（33%）和许可复杂性（37%）仍然是需要专门人才和正式治理结构的障碍。人工智能成为从开源中获益最多的技术领域（38% 的受访者），其采用率从 2024 年起将有 5% 的显著增长。

These findings suggest that organizations that successfully bridge the governance-adoption gap through active engagement, formal structures, and comprehensive security practices capture competitive advantages in talent acquisition, operational excellence, and market positioning, especially in emerging areas such as AI. Organizations that do not treat open source as a strategic ecosystem requiring dedicated investment and expertise risk falling behind competitors who understand that open source involvement has become integral to technological leadership itself.

这些研究结果表明，那些通过积极参与、正式组织结构和全面安全实践成功弥合治理与采纳差距的组织，能够在人才招聘、卓越运营和市场定位方面获得竞争优势，尤其是在人工智能等新兴领域。那些不把开源作为一个需要专门投资和专业知识的战略生态系统的组织，有可能落后于那些了解开源参与已成为技术领先不可或缺的一部分的竞争对手。
