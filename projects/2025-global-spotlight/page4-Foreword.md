# Foreword

# 前言

I’ve been using open source software for well over a decade now, and it’s easy to take for granted the number of high-quality, accessible, and powerful tools that we have at our disposal. It can be hard to remember that for the longest time, there were thousands of businesses and millions of people who had no idea that it existed or that it had the power to completely transform the way their systems and businesses work.

我使用开源软件已经有十多年的时间了，对于我们所拥有的大量高质量、可访问且功能强大的工具，我们很容易认为是理所当然的。我们可能很难回忆起，在过去很长一段时间里，有成千上万的企业和数百万人根本不知道开源软件的存在，也不知道它有能力彻底改变他们的系统和业务运作方式。

Thankfully, that seems to have changed. We’re excited to be collaborating with the team at The Linux Foundation this year on this report, which closely examines the world of open source software to reveal some exciting trends and shifts in the wider landscape. Namely: that open source hasn’t just taken root, but is in full bloom as a mission-critical part of business. This year’s research has gone beyond measuring open source adoption to look at why it’s working, the results enterprises are seeing, and how they’re thinking about the future of open source.

值得庆幸的是，这种情况似乎已经有所改变。今年，我们很高兴能与 Linux 基金会的团队合作撰写这份报告；本报告详细研究了开源软件的世界，揭示了更广领域中一些令人兴奋的趋势和变化。比如开源不仅已经扎根，而且作为业务的关键部分正在全面开花。今年的研究已经超越了对开源软件采用情况的衡量，而是着眼于开源软件发挥作用的原因、企业正在看到的成果，以及他们如何看待开源软件的未来。

And the data reveals what I’ve thought since I first harnessed the power of open source for my own workstations, or to build an on-premises cloud development and deployment platform that was later used by thousands of engineers. Open source is incredible for productivity, and the research this year confirms that: nearly 86% of respondents indicated that using open source software improves productivity. For most developers, this will not be a surprise. I especially love how easy it is to bootstrap a new project with open source software, and the flexibility in tooling and workflow it offers. This has been a personal mission at Canonical since I joined: we want Ubuntu to be the most powerful, flexible foundation for developers. Recently, we’ve spearheaded efforts that focus on ensuring that, no matter what toolchain you use, you’ll be able to find it in Ubuntu.

这些数据揭示了我的想法，自从我第一次利用开源软件的力量为自己的工作站服务，或者建立一个内部部署的云开发和部署平台，后来成千上万的工程师都使用了这个平台。开源软件对提高工作效率有着不可思议的作用，今年的调查也证实了这一点：近 86% 的受访者表示，使用开源软件提高了工作效率。对于大多数开发人员来说，这并不奇怪。我尤其喜欢使用开源软件启动一个新项目是多么容易，以及它所提供的工具和工作流程的灵活性。自从我加入Canonical以来，这一直是我的个人使命：我们希望Ubuntu成为开发者最强大、最灵活的基础。最近，我们率先努力确保无论你使用什么工具链，都能在 Ubuntu 中找到。

It’s not just productivity, but the cost of business too. As the report shows, 46% of organizations reported an increase in business value from open source over the last year. This highlights the impact that open source is making, and I expect we’ll see this number continue to grow over the next few years. We certainly hear the same from customers. In Brazil for example, one of the country’s largest financial institutions has seen remarkable resource efficiency gains from using an open source private cloud, increasing the number of parallel jobs they run in their cloud by more than 400%.

这不仅是生产力的问题，也是业务成本的问题。正如报告所显示的，46% 的组织表示，去年开源带来的业务价值有所增加。这凸显了开源正在产生的影响，我预计这个数字在未来几年还会继续增长。我们当然也从客户那里听到了同样的声音。例如，在巴西，该国最大的金融机构之一通过使用开源私有云获得了显著的资源效率收益，他们在云上运行的并行作业数量增加了 400% 以上。

However, saving some time and money isn’t such a great selling point if it’s not sustainable or safe. More than ever, businesses are demanding more from their OSS in terms of support and security: this research found that more than half of organizations (53%) expect long-term support guarantees, 47% require rapid security patching capabilities, and an eye-opening 71% of organizations expect response times of less than 12 hours from support providers for open source software in production environments. Historically, support has been the hardest thing for OSS to offer, given its business model of passion that relies on grants or donations. But that’s changing: we introduced Ubuntu Pro to address exactly these requirements across a wide range of open source packages and toolchains, and our customers, from space computing pioneers to industry-leading games publishers, value the peace of mind it brings them.

然而，如果不能持续或安全地使用，节省时间和资金就不是一个很好的卖点。在支持和安全方面，企业对开源软件的要求比以往任何时候都更高：这项研究发现，超过一半的企业（53%）希望获得长期的支持保障，47%的企业需要快速的安全补丁功能，而令人大开眼界的是，71%的企业希望生产环境中的开源软件支持提供商的响应时间不超过 12 小时。从历史上看，由于开源软件的商业模式是依靠赠款或捐款，因此支持一直是开源软件最难提供的服务。但是，这种情况正在发生变化：我们推出 Ubuntu Pro 正是为了满足这些需求，并将其广泛应用于开源软件包和工具链中，而我们的客户，从空间计算先驱到行业领先的游戏发行商，都非常看重 Ubuntu Pro 为他们带来的安心。

For me, these findings indicate that we have a long way to go in making organizations aware of best practices for securing their open source supply chain. Research like this is a step in the right direction though. I hope the resources we’re sharing here encourage readers to reflect on the benefits they see from open source software, of which there are many. While it’s important for us to celebrate providing a first-class cloud and developer experience, it’s equally important to pause and reflect on potential gaps in the security of open source supply chains.

对我来说，这些研究结果表明，要让企业认识到保护开源供应链安全的最佳实践，我们还有很长的路要走。不过，这样的研究是朝着正确方向迈出的一步。我希望我们在此分享的资源能鼓励读者反思他们从开源软件中看到的好处，这些好处有很多。对我们来说，为提供一流的云计算和开发者体验而庆祝固然重要，但同样重要的是，停下来反思开源供应链安全方面的潜在漏洞。

JON SEAGER, VP Engineering, Canonical

JON SEAGER, Canonical 工程副总裁
