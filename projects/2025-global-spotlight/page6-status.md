## Widespread adoption, inconsistent management

## 广泛应用，管理不一

**OSS has achieved mission-critical status across enterprise technology stacks**

**开源软件（OSS）在企业技术栈中已达到关键任务（Mission-Critical）地位**

According to our survey respondents, open source has achieved substantial penetration across the core components of enterprise technology stacks (see Figure 1). Operating systems show the highest adoption rate at 55%, reflecting the mature ecosystem around Linux distributions. Cloud and container technologies follow at 49%, with web development, database management, and DevOps clustering between 43% and 46%.

根据我们的受访者反馈，开源软件已经在企业技术栈的核心组件中实现了深度渗透（见图1）。操作系统的采用率最高，达到 55%，这反映了围绕 Linux 发行版的成熟生态系统。云和容器技术紧随其后，为 49%，而 Web 开发、数据库管理和 DevOps 则集中在 43% 到 46% 之间。

These high Open source can be particularly beneficial in emerging technology areas. Open source AI technologies have 40% current adoption, a statistically significant 5% increase from 2024. AI was reported as the technology that benefits the most from being open source (see Figure 3), so we may expect even higher adoption in the future. A previous study from the Linux Foundation shows that open source AI tools provide transparency and cost efficiency. Additionally, open source enables flexible deployment, interoperability, and regulatory assurance.

这些高采用率表明开源在“新兴技术领域”特别有益。开源 AI 技术的当前采用率为 40%，较 2024 年有 5% 的统计学显著增长。AI 被报告为最能从开源模式中受益的技术（见图3），因此我们可以预期未来会有更高的采用率。Linux 基金会此前的一项研究表明，开源 AI 工具提供了透明度和成本效率。此外，开源还实现了灵活部署、互操作性和合规保障。

The importance of open source to AI is widely recognized across regions, with North America (40%) and Asia-Pacific (31%) perceiving AI as the technology that benefits the most from being open source, while Europe considers AI (38%) second to operating systems (43%), as observed in Figure 3.

开源对 AI 的重要性在各地区得到了广泛认可。如图3所示，北美（40%）和亚太地区（31%）将 AI 视为最受益于开源的技术，而欧洲（38%）则认为 AI 仅次于操作系统（43%）。

There is a notable gap between current adoption and future potential of open source cybersecurity tools. While only 33% of respondents currently use open source in cybersecurity (see Figure 1), below all other areas, cybersecurity ranks third among the technologies that would most benefit from open source development (see Figure 3 and Appendix A1 for the full list of 18 technologies).

在开源网络安全工具的“当前采用率”与“未来潜力”之间存在明显差距。虽然目前只有 33% 的受访者在网络安全中使用开源软件（见图1），低于所有其他领域，但在最受益于开源开发模式的技术中，网络安全排名第三（见图3及附录A1中列出的全部18项技术）。

Open source cybersecurity tools offer organizations greater transparency into security mechanisms, enabling independent code audits and community-driven threat detection while reducing dependency on proprietary vendor solutions.

开源网络安全工具为组织提供了更高的安全机制透明度，支持独立的代码审计和社区驱动的威胁检测，同时减少了对专有厂商（Proprietary Vendor）解决方案的依赖。

In terms of industries, information technology, government, and education are viewed as the sectors that benefit most from open source, as observed in Figure 4. The prominence of government and education is particularly noteworthy, suggesting that open source advantages—such as cost savings, transparency, and reduced vendor dependency—are especially relevant in these contexts.

在行业方面，如图4所示，信息技术（IT）、政府和教育被认为是受开源益处最大的领域。政府和教育领域的突出地位特别值得注意，这表明开源的优势——如成本节约、透明度和减少厂商锁定——在这些场景中尤为相关。

Figure 4 also shows that respondents from the government and education sectors place a high value on open source AI, which ranks higher than other technologies, such as operating systems.

图4还显示，来自政府和教育部门的受访者非常看重开源 AI，其排名甚至高于操作系统等其他技术。

These findings indicate that open source has become a strategic technology choice, embedded in the core of the technology stacks. Open source is not supplementary tooling but an ecosystem of core infrastructure dependencies. However, widespread adoption does not necessarily translate to organizational readiness or optimal and secure implementation practices.

这些发现表明，开源已成为一种战略性技术选择，并已嵌入技术栈的核心。开源不再是补充性工具，而是一个由核心基础设施依赖项构成的生态系统。然而，广泛的采用并不一定意味着组织已经做好了准备（Organizational Readiness），也不代表实施了最佳和安全的实践。

**Organizational maturity lags behind adoption rates**

**组织成熟度落后于采用率**

Despite its criticality, Figure 5 reveals a gap in organizational maturity for open source governance. While 34% of organizations have defined a clear open source strategy (up 2% from 2024), only 26% have implemented an Open Source Program Office (OSPO), showing just a 1% increase from the previous year.

尽管开源至关重要，图5揭示了在开源治理方面组织成熟度的差距。虽然 34% 的组织已经制定了清晰的开源战略（较 2024 年增长 2%），但只有 26% 的组织设立了开源项目办公室（OSPO），较上一年仅增长了 1%。

However, this modest growth in traditional OSPOs masks a structural change documented in the 2025 OSPO Report. Organizations are adopting less formalized, less centralized approaches to open source governance due to budget constraints, shifting priorities, and new strategic requirements. Nevertheless, whether centralized or distributed, dedicated open source governance remains critical for managing compliance, security, and contribution workflows.

然而，传统 OSPO 的这种温和增长掩盖了《2025 OSPO 报告》中记录的一种结构性变化。由于预算限制、优先级调整和新的战略需求，组织正在采用不那么正式、不那么集中的开源治理方法。尽管如此，无论是集中式还是分布式，专门的开源治理对于管理合规性（Compliance）、安全性和贡献工作流（Contribution Workflows）仍然至关重要。

**Open source is not supplementary tooling but an ecosystem of core infrastructure dependencies.**

**开源不再是补充性工具，而是一个由核心基础设施依赖项构成的生态系统。**

This lack of clear structures or strategies for open source creates significant risk exposure and limits organizations’ ability to capture the full strategic value of open source participation. Establishing an OSPO helps formalize open source efforts and manage compliance, security, and contribution workflows.

缺乏清晰的开源结构或战略会造成巨大的风险敞口，并限制组织获取参与开源所带来的全部战略价值。建立 OSPO 有助于将开源工作正式化，并管理合规、安全和贡献流程。

These offices provide policies and guidelines, fostering responsible engagement while addressing legal and licensing concerns. OSPOs also play a pivotal role in increasing contributions by empowering employees with resources and support to participate in open source projects.

这些办公室提供政策和指南，在促进负责任的参与同时，解决法律和许可证问题。OSPO 在增加贡献方面也发挥着关键作用，通过为员工提供资源和支持，赋能他们参与到开源项目中。

**The lack of clear structures or strategies for open source creates significant risk exposure and limits organizations’ ability to capture the full strategic value of open source participation.**

**缺乏清晰的开源结构或战略会造成巨大的风险敞口，并限制组织获取参与开源所带来的全部战略价值。**

This governance challenge manifests differently across organizational sizes, though strategic clarity remains universally lacking. As Figure 6 illustrates, large enterprises (10,000+ employees) are 2.4 times more likely to have OSPOs compared to small companies (39% vs. 16%), reflecting their greater resources, complex compliance needs, and ability to dedicate specialized teams to open source governance.

这种治理挑战在不同规模的组织中表现不同，尽管战略清晰度的缺乏是普遍存在的。如图6所示，大型企业（10,000名以上员工）拥有 OSPO 的可能性是小公司的 2.4 倍（39% 对比 16%），这反映了它们拥有更多的资源、复杂的合规需求以及组建专门团队进行开源治理的能力。

However, all organizations, regardless of size, show similar low rates of defined public positions on open source, hovering between 26% and 27%. Having a defined public position provides many benefits: it signals commitment to developers and partners, attracts talent who value open source culture, clarifies contribution policies for employees, and builds trust within the open source community. Without such positions, organizations miss opportunities to differentiate themselves in competitive talent markets.

然而，无论规模大小，所有组织在“明确的开源公开立场”方面都表现出相似的低比例，徘徊在 26% 到 27% 之间。拥有明确的公开立场有很多好处：它向开发者和合作伙伴表明承诺，吸引认同开源文化的人才，为员工阐明贡献政策，并在开源社区内建立信任。如果没有这样的立场，组织就会错失在竞争激烈的人才市场中脱颖而出的机会。

Regional variations also emerge, with Asia-Pacific leading OSPO implementation at 36%, followed by North America at 30% and Europe at 22%. Europe’s lower rates across both metrics (22% for OSPOs, 22% for public positions) may reflect different regulatory environments, cultural approaches to open source, or varying stages of digital transformation. For a deeper analysis of the European scenario, we refer the reader to the segmented analysis for this region presented in the report “Open Source as Europe’s Strategic Advantage.”

区域差异也随之出现，亚太地区在 OSPO 实施方面处于领先地位，达到 36%，其次是北美（30%）和欧洲（22%）。欧洲在这两项指标上的较低比率（OSPO 为 22%，公开立场为 22%）可能反映了不同的监管环境、对开源的文化态度或数字化转型的不同阶段。关于欧洲情况的更深入分析，我们建议读者参考报告《开源作为欧洲的战略优势》中针对该区域的细分分析。
